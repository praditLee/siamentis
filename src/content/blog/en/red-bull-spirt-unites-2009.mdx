"Red Bull Spirit Unites!" 2009 is the first gathering of all Red Bull Spirit volunteers, after the successful completion of the first year of the program. The two-day event was held on the 17th and 18th of January, at Muak Lek, Saraburi.

Over 300 participants came to participate in a number of volunteer activities in the morning and enjoy a concert in the evening. A press conference was held on the following morning to announce activities planned for 2009.
