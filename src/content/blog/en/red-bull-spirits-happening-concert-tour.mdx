Red Bull Spirit joins [Special Olympics Thailand](http://www.specialolympicsthai.com/triplesystems/index.php?newlang=english) and Happening magazine to organize "Red Bull Spirit Happening Concert" to promote volunteer activities and to attract students to volunteer in the upcoming National Special Olympic Games to be held in Bangkok between 26 - 29 August 2008.

The concert tour features "Friday" and "Scrubb" bands. The two bands will play at the following venues:

- Srinakarinwiroj University, Prasarnmitr Campus on 21 July 2008, at 17:00 hr.
- Thammasat University, Rangsit Campus, on 15 August 2008, at 17:00 hr.
- Kasetsart University on 21 August 2008, at 17:00 hr.
