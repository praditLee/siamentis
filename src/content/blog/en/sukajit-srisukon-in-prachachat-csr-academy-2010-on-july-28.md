![](/images/sukajit1.jpg) Sukajit Srisukon, Managing Partner and Creative Director of Siamentis will be sharing her experiences in CSR program management and CSR program communications with participants in Prachachat CSR Academy 2010. ?Sukajit and her fellow speaker form Total Access Communications Plc., (or DTAC) will be hosting their session on "Enhancing Brands with CSR" at the workshop on July 28, 2010.

The workshop will be held on the 9th Floor of [Matichon](http://maps.google.co.th/maps?f=q&source=s_q&hl=en&q=%E0%B8%A1%E0%B8%95%E0%B8%B4%E0%B8%8A%E0%B8%99&sll=13.836137,100.546365&sspn=0.00423,0.004726&ie=UTF8&split=1&rq=1&ev=zi&radius=0.19&hq=%E0%B8%A1%E0%B8%95%E0%B8%B4%E0%B8%8A%E0%B8%99&hnear=&ll=13.836137,100.546365&spn=0.00423,0.004726&z=18&iwloc=A) Building. For more information and registration, visit [www.prachachart.net](http://www.prachachat.net/) or call 0 2580 0021 ext 1505, 1544.
