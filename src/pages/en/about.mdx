Siamentis Limited was founded in 2002 and we have worked with corporations, educational institutions, and various organizations to bring about change through education, experience design, communication, and innovation.

For the first 10 years since its inception, Siamentis primarily focused on utilizing creative communication strategies to influence the perceptions and behaviors of its target audiences. Examples of our past projects include:

- Studying problems and designing intervention programs in partnership with leading universities to improve nutrition for children aged 6 to 24 months.

- Assisting universities in enhancing their communication strategies to effectively engage and attract prospective students.

- Designing and implementing impactful Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) programs to achieve various goals, such as fostering volunteerism among members of the Thai public, training entrepreneurs in financial literacy and business model generation, and creating public spaces that promote physical health through exercise.

Beginning in 2013, our company has been dedicated to improving the education system in Thailand. We began with a [multi-year school-based program](https://siamentis.com/samsung-smart-learning-center/), funded by the private sector, to equip students with essential 21st century soft skills and develop a learning model that could fit the needs and constraints of schools in Thailand. At the same time, we upgraded classrooms and built the capacity of teachers to facilitate this program.

The program’s philosophy has inspired teachers and students, its methods have improved teaching effectiveness, and the workshops pushed the teachers to incorporate new bodies of knowledge, teach differently, and step out of their comfort zone. They no longer relied solely on lectures to transfer information but became facilitators of learning for the students.

Students learned via trying to answer a question they propose to themselves while teachers created an open and lively environment for them. The classroom became a new, flexible space that can be transformed to suit different tasks while learning became fun and empowering.

Our program continues to evolve with the changing technology landscape.

As student projects have become increasingly sophisticated, incorporating technologies like coding, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, our services have evolved to provide more intensive, personalized support to individuals or small teams.This evolution includes offering online courses to broaden our reach, as well as providing training, coaching, and mentoring to guide students through the complexities of their projects, from initial concept to the development of a viable prototype, as exemplified by programs like [Samsung Solve for Tomorrow](https://siamentis.com/samsung-solve-for-tomorrow/).
