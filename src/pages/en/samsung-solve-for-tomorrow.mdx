The [Samsung Solve for Tomorrow](http://www.solvefortomorrow.in.th) competition challenges students to submit impactful project ideas annually. As the organizing partner of the "Make Your Impact" themed competition, we conduct Design Thinking and Business Model Development courses to equip students with the skills needed to sharpen their project ideas. Additionally, we provide coaching and mentoring support to help students bring their projects from the idea phase to a viable prototype.
