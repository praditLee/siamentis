Despite the one-day camp being held twice, the camp’s cutting-edge content attracted over twice the number of available slots. From leading public schools to vocational schools, the camp offered education on a subject that many have heard of, but few understand, and even fewer are able to teach.

The feedback was overwhelmingly positive: the camp’s unique blend of active learning, group activities, and content tailored to two different groups of audience that are taught concurrently is a first for the participants. The camp reinforces Samsung as a key player in consumer electronics and its position as a leading technology company.
